I became interested in front-end development because I wanted to understand how these interfaces were created. I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript because of my passion, and quickly fell in love with web development. I enjoy combining creativity with problem-solving, and front-end development allows me to do just that. I am motivated to become proficient in modern frameworks like React.js and Next.js to build highly interactive and performance-driven web applications.
